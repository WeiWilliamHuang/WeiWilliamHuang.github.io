I am Wei Huang, a Lecturer and master supervisor Ocean University of China in the Faculty of Information Science and Engineering, Ocean University of China (2021-now). I graduated from the School of Electronic Information, Wuhan University, majoring in the Electronic Information Science and Technology. In the same year, I majored in Communication Engineering at the School of Electronic Information, Wuhan University as a joint master's and doctoral student, and graduated in 2021.
My research interests are primarily focused on underwater sound speed field construction and prediction, underwater communication and localization, underwater signal processing, machine learning. I have published more than 30 SCI/EI papers, including 15 SCI papers as first/corresponding author. I have been granted by 8 Chinese invention patents. I was awarded the Best Paper Award at the 2024 Signal Processing and Communication Technology (SPCT) International Conference. I served as a reviewer for many journals such as IEEE JOE, IEEE TCCN, Applied Ocean Research, Remote Sensing, and as a TPC member for GLOBECOM 2024 Workshop ISCEI (Workshop on Integrated Sensing and Communications for Edge Intelligence). One of the first graduate students I supervised have won the 2024 National Scholarship.
